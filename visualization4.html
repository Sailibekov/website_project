Parallel Coordinates Plot - D3

Uncovering Patterns of Top Contributors

Key Insights

  • Strong Impact of Attendance and Study Hours:

    As the correlation matrix shows, attendance and hours studied have a great effect on current exam scores. Most of the lines representing students with high attendance and high study hours trend toward high exam scores.

  • Limited Influence of Previous Scores:

    In contrast, previous scores appear to have little direct effect on current exam performance. The plot shows that lines passing through the previous score axis can end up at a wide range of exam scores, from low to high.
