Project Conclusion

Final Summary

In summary, the visualizations have taught us that while multiple factors affect student performance, ensuring high attendance and promoting dedicated study habits are critical. The insights gathered here can guide educational institutions in designing more effective interventions. Looking ahead, expanding the dataset and refining analytical methods will further enhance our understanding of academic outcomes, ultimately paving the way for smarter, data-driven decision-making in education.

Key Insights

Attendance and study hours emerged as the most influential factors, strongly correlating with improved exam scores.

Actionable Strategies

Targeted interventions, such as supporting attendance through encouragement or even mandatory policies, coupled with increased study hours, are significantly associated with improved student performance.

Future Directions

Future research should refine predictive models and expand data collection,by gathering larger, real-world datasets, to further optimize educational policies and resource allocation.
